Google Apps

Google originally started as a search engine website. It now has grown into one of the most innovative companies in history!

My Favorite Google Apps

Google has been adding apps to their website constantly. Listed below are some of my favorite

Google Colaboratory: Google Colaboratory is a cloud service that allows you to run code on remote machines that are more powerful. It is commonly used with Python Tensorflow

Google Firebase: Firebase is a cloud computing service that allows you to easily deploy and manage your web applications.

Google Drive: Google Drive is a cloud storage solution that offers 15 GB of free storage per account.
